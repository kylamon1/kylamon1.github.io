About me

My name is Shawn Henry.

I am have been teaching middle school 7th and 8th grade mathematics since 2006. I hobbies include video games, coding, reading and spending time with my family.

Below are some the games I've made. They are playable right on your device by clicking the image.

RollyPauly LowRes

This is a remake of a game I made called RollyPauly. The original game was programed in Java over about 5 years and was available on android phones, but I never updated the game so it got removed. This new remake is programed using puzzlescript and was made in about 3-4 days. It does not look near as pretty, but it functions almost exactly the same as the original. Click the image to play.

Controls:
On a computer: Move with arrow keys, Undo with Z, Reset level with R
On phone/tablet: Move with swipe, Undo or Reset level using sliding menu in top left of screen(you must slide it out first)

RollyPauly Adventure

This is a new game made off the same premice, but the idea was a more open world adventure and less constrained to individual levels. The goal is the collect the 7 keys and get through the door. This was also programed in puzzlescript and most the the planning came in making the world. I was able to beat this little adventure in about 2 minutes. Can you even finish? Click the image to start your adventure.

Controls:
On a computer: Move with arrow keys, Undo with Z, Reset to last checkpoint with R
On phone/tablet: Move with swipe, Undo or Reset to last checkpoint using sliding menu in top left of screen(you must slide it out first)

Pull the Crates

This is a sokoban style game, but instead of pushing the crates you pull them. I added a few other features like conveyor belts, doors, water, etc.

Controls:
On a computer: Move with arrow keys, Undo with Z, Reset to level with R
On phone/tablet: Move with swipe, Undo or Reset level using sliding menu in top left of screen(you must slide it out first)
